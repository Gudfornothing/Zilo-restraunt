Nutrient-Rich Ingredients

Fruits : Cakes made with fruits such as apples, bananas, or berries can provide essential vitamins and antioxidants. For example, berries are rich in vitamins C and K and provide beneficial antioxidants.

Nuts : Nut-based cakes can offer protein, healthy fats, and minerals such as magnesium and potassium. Almonds, walnuts, and hazelnuts, for instance, contribute to heart health and provide essential nutrients.

Whole Grains : Cakes made with whole grain flours, such as whole wheat or oats, can provide dietary fiber, which aids digestion and helps maintain healthy cholesterol levels.
